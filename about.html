Who Are We?

Sipho Mkhwane was born on the 15 September 1986. He matriculated at Sihlengiwe High School in the 2006, He furthered his studies at Denver Technical College and secured a qualification in Civil Engineering N4 . He then became a building inspector for PDS Civil Engineering from 2006 , he was also a project manager for Thasma solutions from 2015 till 2017.

Mr Mkhwane he is very passionate and he is much respected man in the community in building Construction( Building, Electricity, Plumbing, Raft Foundation, Aluminium, Painting , Plastering , Floor Tilling, Roofing) transport, catering, stationary and cleaning. With such expertise the director is leading the empire into vibrancy.

Sipho Mkwane trading was registered with the Commissioner of Companies and Intellectual Property Commission on the 21st of February 2012. The director of the company is Sipho Evans Mkhwane. The private company was formed with an intention of providing a variety of service to industries and caring out any project within their capability. The driving forces that led to the entry of this venture is because the member saw that South Africa as a country is highly industrialised and his wish is to contribute to the industries as a service provider. The private company aims to create value to the business by achieving growth in the manner that is economically, environmentally and socially suitable over the long term.
